My name is Kyle. I am currently a PhD student studying Computer Science I am a PhD student studying Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago and a research assistant in the Electronic Visualization Laboratory, Creative Coding research group. My primary research focus involves multi-temporal volume rendering and visualization of neuroimaging data, however my interests extend to Information Visualization, Language Development, and function Magnetic Resonance Imaging.

I received my Bachelors degree in Speech, Language, and Hearing Sciences at the University of Arizona and spent the better part of a decade working as a Research Technician in the Plante Lab directed by Dr. Elena Plante where we utilized fMRI to explore how the brain processes language. Now I am developing new and interesting ways of exploring neuroimaging data to better understand the complexities of the human brain.
